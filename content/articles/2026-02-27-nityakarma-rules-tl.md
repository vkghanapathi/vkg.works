preamble: Nityakarma constitutes the obligatory daily rituals prescribed in the Vedic
  tradition for maintaining ritual purity, spiritual discipline, and cosmic order
  (ṛta). These practices form the foundation of Hindu religious life, spanning sandhyāvandana
  (twilight worship), agnihotra (fire offerings), and other daily observances. Dr.
  Ghanapāṭhī's analysis grounds these prescriptions in classical dharmaśāstra texts
  and Vedic exegesis, making their underlying principles and contemporary application
  intelligible to scholarly readers.
