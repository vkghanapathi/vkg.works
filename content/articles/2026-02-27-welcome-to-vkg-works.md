Welcome to vkg.works — the online home of Dr. Vamśīkṛṣṇa Ghanapāṭhī.

This site presents original scholarship in Vedic recitation, Sanskrit poetry, and traditional learning. Content is organized into:

- **Articles** — administrative, technical, and analytical writings
- **Poems** — Sanskrit and Kannada poetry
- **Songs** — compositions with audio recordings
- **Books** — full-length publications
- **Audio** — recordings of Vedic recitation
- **Video** — lectures and performances
- **Projects** — ongoing scholarly work
- **Live** — upcoming events and live streams
- **Coverage** — press and event documentation
