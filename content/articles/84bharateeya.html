84. Who am I? A Bhaarateeya or Hindu?

Who am I?

A Bhaarateeya or Hindu?

Technically Correct - Bhaarateeya

(Not Hindu)

Just observe our Constitutional documents and Government documents correctly depict this phenomenon as BHAARAT(a) ....

This is a geographical representation as well as cultural/ traditional.

Hindu and India are references from the western visitors...

Closer neighbours called as Sindhu region - Hindu. (S is replaced by H in persian language).

INDIES is the land between sea.

Therefore western expeditions named islands, Peninsula and archipelago as Indies.

East Indies (Malaysia, Indonesia, etc.

West Indies - Caribbean islands

-----------

Read the traditional description for our region/ nation/ continent ....

Broadly speaking, the region between Eastern Ghats and Western Ghats abutting the sea coastal lines is the PENINSULA that is called as Aaryaavarta.

---

Madhya Desha is the region between the Saraswati Basin and Drushadwati River...

Madhya Desh = Below Himayala and above Vindhya; with Vinashana (Disappearing point of Saraswati to the west and Prayaga to the East).

----------

Brahmaavarta is the region betwwen the coordinates of

Kuru Kshetra - Haryana

Matsya - Jaipur (Rajasthan) area

Shura Senaka - Mathura Region

Panchaala - Foot Hills of Himalaya

------

Vedic references and Purana versions speak about Bharata Varsha and Khanda ...

Brahmaavarta, Aaryaavarta, Madhya Desha ..

This is the collection of verses from Maha Bharata, reproduced in Manu Smruti as well.

आसमुद्राच्च यत् पूर्वदासमुद्राच्च पश्चिमात्।

हिमाद्रिविन्ध्पयोर्मध्ये आर्यावर्तं प्रचक्षते॥ आश्व.९२ दा.पा.अ.XV॥

सरस्वती दृषद्वत्योर्देवनद्योर्यदन्तरम्।

तद्देवनिर्मितं देशं ब्रह्मावर्तं प्रचक्षते॥ आश्व.९२ दा.पा.अ.XV॥

कुरुक्षेत्रं च मत्स्याश्च पञ्चालाः शूरसेनयः।

एते ब्रह्मर्षिदेशास्तु ब्रह्मावर्तादनन्तराः॥ आश्व.९२ दा.पा.अ.XV॥

मध्यदेश -

हिमवद्विन्ध्ययोर्मध्यं यत्प्राग्विशसनादपि।

प्रत्यगेव प्रयागात् तु मध्यदेशः प्रकीर्तितः॥ आश्व.९२ दा.पा.अ.XV॥

कृष्णसारस्तु चरति मृगो यत्र स्वभावतः।

स ज्ञेयो याज्ञिको देशो म्लेच्छदेशस्ततः परम्॥ आश्व.९२ दा.पा.अ.XV॥
