The Vedic Recitation Archive is a long-term project to document and preserve authentic Rigveda Ghanapatha recitation as transmitted in the traditional lineage.

## Objectives

- Record and archive Ghanapatha recitations in full
- Document recitation rules (vikṛtis) in textual form
- Make recordings freely available for researchers and practitioners

## Status

Ongoing. New recordings added periodically.
